# Risk Register (MVP-B2)

## R1: Disintermediation (outside deals)
- Likelihood: High
- Impact: High
- Mitigation:
  - Deal pack + completion docs (value artifact)
  - Verified badges + ranking signals
  - Keep supplier contact hidden until award/confirmation
  - Audit trail and dispute support

## R2: Supplier non-response (no offers)
- Likelihood: Medium
- Impact: High
- Mitigation:
  - n8n SLA nudges/escalation
  - Supplier onboarding playbook + templates
  - Multi-supplier broadcast per RFQ

## R3: Arabic/RTL QA issues
- Likelihood: Medium
- Impact: Medium
- Mitigation:
  - Translation keys enforcement
  - RTL “component torture page”
  - B2 QA week

## R4: Ops complexity (mobilisation, operator, disputes)
- Likelihood: Medium
- Impact: High
- Mitigation:
  - Keep MVP workflow structured (line items, constraints)
  - Deal artifacts + checklists
  - Status timeline + clear cancellation rules

## R5: Demo risk (downtime)
- Likelihood: Medium
- Impact: High
- Mitigation:
  - Upgrade Supabase Pro + Vercel Pro before demos/pilots
  - Monitoring + simple health checks
