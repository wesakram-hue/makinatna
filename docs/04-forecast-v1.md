# Forecast v1 — RFQ Procurement Model (Adjustable)

## Forecast method
We forecast using the procurement funnel:
RFQs → RFQs with offers → Awards (jobs) → Completed jobs → Revenue

## Inputs (edit as we learn)
- RFQs/month
- Offer rate (% RFQs receiving ≥1 offer)
- Award rate (% RFQs awarded)
- Completion rate (% awarded completed)
- Avg job value (SAR)
- Take rate (% success fee on awarded/completed jobs)
- Optional add-ons (later): transport coordination, compliance pack, guarantee

## Base-case Year 1 (illustrative)
- Total RFQs: 1,200
- Offer rate (avg): 70%
- Award rate (avg): 20%
- Completion rate: 80%
- Avg job value: SAR 18,000
- Take rate: 8%

### Outputs (base case)
- RFQs with offers: 840
- Awards (jobs): 240
- Completed: 192
- GMV (awarded): 4.32M SAR
- Revenue (on awarded @8%): 346k SAR

- Completion rate
